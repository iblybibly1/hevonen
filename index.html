Our Story
Lux Hill Stables

Lux Hill Stables was born from a need to create a serene, world-class home for horses — and a desire to offer the finest equestrian experience in the region.

Nestled among rolling hills, our estate spans over 120 acres of pristine countryside. We offer elite boarding, professional training, and riding tuition for all levels — always rooted in a deep respect for the horse.

Explore Our Stables
01 — Boarding

Premium care in a setting that honours the horse's natural spirit.

Our private stables offer individually tailored full-care boarding — personalised nutrition, daily turnout on lush paddocks, and round-the-clock attentive care from our dedicated team.

We limit our boarding numbers to ensure every resident receives the attention they deserve.

02 — Training

Professional programmes for young horses and competition athletes.

From foundational groundwork for young horses to advanced preparation for competition, our certified trainers design bespoke programmes that develop each horse's full potential.

Our 60x20m arena and varied outdoor terrain provide the ideal environment for progressive, thoughtful training.

About Lux Hill Stables

A sanctuary built on passion, precision, and a profound respect for the horse.

Lux Hill Stables was established with a singular vision: to create a place where horses live their best lives and riders reach their full potential.

Over fifteen years, we have cultivated a community of passionate equestrians, world-class facilities, and a culture of excellence that extends to every corner of our estate.
